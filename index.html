About Monster Survivors

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an engaging survival game that puts your strategic thinking and reflexes to the test. As a lone survivor, you must fend off waves of increasingly challenging monsters while collecting resources and upgrading your abilities.

Key Features

  • • Intense wave-based combat system
  • • Character progression and upgrades
  • • Dynamic monster AI
  • • Smooth controls and responsive gameplay
  • • Cross-platform compatibility

How to Play

Controls

  • • WASD or Arrow Keys - Move character
  • • Mouse - Aim and shoot
  • • Space - Special ability
  • • E - Interact with objects

Objectives

  • • Survive waves of monster attacks
  • • Collect resources and power-ups
  • • Upgrade your character's abilities
  • • Unlock new weapons and skills
